More than just training


At Energenic you work with the best personal trainers to achieve your physical goals. Personal training at Energenic stands for:


A personal approach


At Energenic personal training is really personal. Per person we make a completely tailor-made program, tailored to your goals. Our personal trainers are fully involved in your process and will not only coach you during training and coaching sessions, but will also regularly ask you how you are doing and support you where necessary.


Coaching on your new lifestyle


We know from experience that in order to achieve long-term results it is important to look at several lifestyle aspects. In addition to the training sessions, our trainers will also look with you at your diet, how to improve sleep, how to improve your work-life balance, how to breathe and how to manage your stressors. Through specific coaching in these areas you will not only look better, but you will also feel better!

Health dates and the Bodyreset


What is unique is that during the programme we monitor your health data such as your heart rate, blood pressure, blood sugar level and HRV (Heart rate variability). This gives you a better understanding of the effect of your lifestyle on your health. In addition, we use this data to make the training sessions even more effective and to monitor progress accurately so that we can make adjustments quickly when necessary. A fixed part of our programmes is the Bodyreset. During this treatment, our physiotherapists test and improve the control of your muscles and joints. This way, we know exactly what points require attention during training and we remove any obstacles as much as possible. Our programme may sound like a lot of work, but this investment of time pays off in greater effectiveness and thus a faster result!
